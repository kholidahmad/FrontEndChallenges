Raja Ampat

This is one of the prima donna of the most superior in the world of Indonesian tourism. Its natural beauty has made this tourist area widely recognized, both by domestic and foreign tourists. Raja Ampat itself is a collection of islands at the tip of Papua. There are four main islands here, namely Batanta, Misool, Salawati, and Waigeo. The underwater wealth of Raja Ampat is no longer in doubt.
This is because the waters in Raja Ampat are known as one of the best snorkeling and diving locations in the world. If you want to come here, come in October and November which are said to be the best times because the weather and water conditions are ideal. In addition to exploring the underwater beauty, you can also enjoy other charms in this place, from stopping at residents' houses, buying handicrafts, and so on.

Bromo Tengger

Turning to East Java, there is one tourist destination that you really need to visit while on vacation. Mount Bromo, one of the active volcanoes, is located at an altitude of 2,329 meters above sea level. This mountain has become one of the proud destinations for East Java residents with its exotic volcanic mountain views.
Mount Bromo does save beautiful natural scenery. Here you will see the beauty of nature from a height and from various angles. There are many things you can enjoy the view here, such as the Teletubbies hill, the stretch of the Bromo sand sea, and a number of beautiful craters.
